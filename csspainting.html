KA2 by Bridget Riley

When I first saw KA2 by Bridget Riley, it reminded me of the lines of color on a television screen. I remember when I was a kid, we had an old box TV from the 90's. If we had the input connected to the dvd player but accidentally pressed the channel flipper, it would show the bright stripes of color. I thought it was interesting that this was a painting, and yet it reminded me of something digital. I chose a black background to contrast with these bright colors, and make it feel more digital.
