The Mysterious Island

What Will We Find?

We have been sailing for many days when we see an island. Is that where the treasure is buried?



We step foot on the island and begin following the map's path. It leads us to the center of the island where there is a clearing. The map says that this is where the treasure is buried. Click the shovel to start digging!
